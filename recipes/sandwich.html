How to Make a Sandwich

Sandwiches are a staple item in our house and probably yours. But don't let one of your family's go to dishes get stale by serving the same thing night after night

This recipe is a little different and for sure will have your kids asking for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Bread - Get some good bread
  • Enough Cheese to cover the bread - Get some good cheese, we like sharp cheddar
  • Heat - Get some good heat

Steps

  1. Butter one side of both pieces of bread, place the buttered side face down in a hot pan - not too hot!
  2. Place the cheese on top of the bread, making sure that the distribution over the bread is even. You don't want piles of cheese!
  3. Close the sandwich by placing the second piece of bread on top of the first, buttered side up!
  4. Flip once or twice. Pay careful attention not to burn the bread.
  5. Your sandwich is ready when the cheese is melty.
