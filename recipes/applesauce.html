Apple Sauce

How to Make Apple Sauce

The first step to making apple sauce is picking the right apples. You should try to find a variety of apples, both green and red, but most importantly in season and delicious. Delicious apples make delicious apple sauce!

This particular recipe is as simple as it gets. Apples, lemon juice, a little water, cinnamon, brown sugar, and that's it. We love it warm and spooned over ice cream.
